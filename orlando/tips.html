Magic Kingdom Tips

Planning and Touring Strategies

  • Prioritize must-dos before you go.

  • Find out when parades and fireworks are scheduled.

  • Arrive at rope drop.

  • Seven Dwarfs Mine Train, Peter Pan and Rise of the Resistance are popular rope drop attractions.

  • Lines die down after fireworks. Stay in the park until closing.

  • Lines are shortest for the two hours after the park opens and after fireworks in the evening.

  • If you are in line when the park closes, you can still wait in the queue until you board the ride.

  • Shops on Main Street stay open an hour after park closing.

  • A good place to get away from crowds and relax is to stand on the boardwalk on the river behind the Turkey Leg and Churro stands in Frontierland.

  • when leaving, avoid the first monorail on the right (Transportation and Ticket Center monorail). Instead, take the Resort Monorail as the second stop is Transportation and Ticket Center.

Tips on when to visit

  • Avoid Magic Kingdom on a Monday.

  • Pay attention to holiday party dates.

Tips for Rides and Attractions

  • You can request front or back row for certain rides.

  • If Haunted Mansion is at a 13 minute wait, it may actually be a 5 minute wait or even a walk on.

  • Stand under the painting of the woman holding a parasol on a tightrope in Haunted Mansion's stretching room to board the ride quicker.

  • The Hall of Presidents is good to cool off if needed.

  • Main street vehicles only run in the morning.

  • Tom Sawyer Island closes before dark.

Lightning Lane and Genie+ Tips

  • Genie+ is a must for this park.

  • Rides to prioritize with Genie+:

    • Space Mountain

    • Peter Pan's Flight

    • Jungle Cruise

Parade Tips

  • Know the parade route. It starts by Splash Mountain/Tiana's in Fontierland, runs through part of Liberty Square then passes Cinderella's Castle and goes down Main Street. It ends in Town Square.

  • Most people line up along Main Street. There is less of a crowd in Frontierland.

  • Plan to eat at Liberty Tree Tavern so you are done 20 minutes before the parade starts. Step out on the porch as the parade comes by.

  • The balcony of the Magic Kingdom train station when you enter the park is a good viewpoint. Try to get there 30 minutes before the parade starts.

  • Try to navigate Main Street during the parade by walking through the connected shops.

  • Ride wait times can dip during parades.

  • Cavalcades are shorter parades.

Fireworks Tips

  • Consider watching the fireworks in the hub or on Main Street to see the projections and pyrotechnics.

Dining Tips

  • Dole Whip can be found in Adventureland at Aloha Isle.

  • Skipper Canteen and Liberty Tree Tavern are two easy dining reservations at good restaurants.

  • Avoid Tony's Town Square.

Hollywood Studios Tips

Planning and Touring Strategies

  • Arrive to the park 45-60 minutes before park opening.

  • Rope drop Rise of the Resistance or Slinky Dog Dash.

  • If you can get Lightning Lanes for Rise or Slinky, it may be better to head to Tower of Terror, then Rock 'n' Roller Coaster.

  • Beat the heat by watching indoor shows.

  • Shops stay open one hour after the last attraction has closed.

Tips for Rides and Attractions

  • If the wait time for Tower of Terror is 13 minutes, head there ASAP to have a minimal wait.

  • When you enter the first library at Tower of Terror, go to the far corner (furthest from the door you enter). After the TV presentation, the wall opens up to the rest of the right. Go in the left line.

Lightning Lane and Genie+ Tips

  • Prioritize Slinky Dog Dash for a Lightning Lane.

  • Buy an individual Lightning Lane for Rise of the Resistance sooner rather than later.

Galaxy's Edge Tips

  • Use mobile ordering at Docking Bay 7, Ronto Roasters and the Milk Stand.

Dining Tips

  • Consider the Fantasmic dining package available at Brown Derby, Mama Melrose or Hollywood & Vine. The show can be hard to get into, but the package offers priority seating.

Epcot Tips

Planning and Touring Strategies

  • Not everything opens at park open. World Celebration, World Nature and World Discovery open at park open, while the majority of World Showcase opens at 11am.

Tips for what to do

  • Explore all the shops in World Showcase.

  • Explore everything each country has to offer.

  • Don't miss Morocco or Mexico.

  • Check out the live entertainment.

  • Check out the aquarium in The Seas with Nemo and Friends pavillion in World Nature.

Tips on when to visit

  • Avoid festival weekends.

  • Avoid runDisney Race weekends.

  • Avoid the first few days of festivals.

Tips for Rides and Attractions

  • Remy's, Frozen Ever After, Test Track and Soarin' are the most popular rides. They will have the shortest lines at the beginning of the day and the end of the day.

  • Guardians of the Galaxy is currently virtual queue or individual lightning lanes.

  • Be sure to do the Green Mission at Mission Space before the Orange Mission.

  • Ask cast member to sit in the top middle of Soarin'. It may be a longer wait but it is a better experience.

Lightning Lane and Genie+ Tips

  • Epcot doesn't have a lot of rides, so Genie+ may not be necessary unless you want to save time on Frozen Ever After, Test Track, Mission Space or Soarin'.

  • The top three rides to prioritize are Frozen, Test Track and Soarin'.

Fireworks Tips

  • Pay attention to the wind and go where the the wind is blowing so smoke won't blow into your face.

Dining Tips

  • For dining, book Space 220 or Le Cellier as early as possible.

  • Easier to get reservations that are worthwhile include Chefs de France, Tutto Italia and Biergarten.

  • Check out Club Cool to sample Coke products.

  • The long line at Les Halles Boulangerie & Patisserie in France is worth the wait as it is a really good bakery.

  • For fish and chips, avoid the Rose and Crown Pub. Go to Yorkshire County Fish Shop (the food stand next to the pub). It's cheaper and faster.

  • If you want to get out of the park for a bit, you can head to the Boardwalk for dining options.

  • Use mobile ordering for Regal Eagle Smokehouse, Sommerfest and Yorkshire Country Fish Shop.

Festival Tips

  • Bring a tray if there is a food festival. Rather than buying one, get fish and chips from Yorkshire, save the tray and use it for the festival.

  • Set a food budget during festivals. Items can be between $3-$9 and costs can get out of hand.

  • Get the Festival passport when you enter the park. It will give you menus for all booths.

Animal Kingdom Tips

Planning and Touring Strategies

  • Plan your day around the big attractions but explore the trails and pay attention to the details.

  • Keep the earlier park closing in mind when planning.

  • Crowds are lowest during the morning.

  • Animals are most active in the morning and seek shade in the afternoon if it is hot. Check out the animal attractions first.

  • Animal Kingdom is hotter and more humid than other parks.

  • Many paths are uneven, so bring good shoes.

  • Visit attractions during the day and at night. Kilimanjaro Safaris use special lighting and different animals at night, the Tree of Life has projections when it gets dark, Pandora lights up with bioluminescence.

Tips for Rides and Attractions

  • Flight of Passage can cause motion sickness.

  • Try to get a seat at the end of your row on Kilimanjaro Safaris for the best pictures.

  • Head to Kilimanjaro Safaris if it rains as animals come out during rainy weather.

  • There is a secret path. When heading to Harambe Village, keep your eye out for the last path to the right after the Tree of Life and before the bridge to Africa. the front of the path is filled with a lot of vegetation, but follow it for a close look at the Tree of Life.

  • Secure bags (especially on Dinosaur) as they are known to fly off the ride.

  • Bring plastic bags to put phones in when on Kali River Rapids or any other water rides.

  • Keep your feel above the silver rings near the bottom of the raft on Kali River Rapids to keep your feet somewhat dry.

  • Bring sandals and take off shoes/socks before going on water rides.

Lightning Lane and Genie+ Tips

  • Genie+ isn't necessary for Animal Kingdom unless it's offered at a low cost.

  • Genie+ priorities should include: Expedition Everest, Navi River Journey and Kilimanjaro Safaris.

  • Never buy an individual lightning lane for Expedition Everest but consider it for Flight of Passage.

Dining Tips

  • A good spot to eat or take a break is behind Flame Tree Barbecue. Follow the path to the left of the counter.

  • Get into the park early by making breakfast reservations.

  • Nomad Lounge offers a spot to sit by the water.

  • Some of the best eateries are Satuli Canteen, Tiffins and Skippers Canteen.

Time Saving Tips

How to Save Time in Line at Disney World

  • Many attractions have two lines (right and left). If you can't see the end of the line, go left as most people subconsciously pick the right line.

  • Lines are shortest in the morning and evening. Get to the park at least 30-45 minutes before open and prioritize Remy's Ratatouille Adventure, Smuggler's Run, Frozen Ever After, Flight of Passage and Rise of the Resistance.

  • Use Lightning Lanes even if the park is slow.

  • Jump into a queue before the park closes so you can get one last ride in.

  • Shop after the park closes as they stay open one hour after park closure.

  • Enjoy Epcot festival booths early in the day.

  • If you can't get early park entry, you may want to avoid rope dropping the most popular rides as the queues will already be long.

  • After entering the first staging room (library) in the Tower of Terror, go to the far corner. After the TV presentation, a wall opens up to the rest of the ride. Choose the left line.

  • When you enter the room with the fireplace in the Haunted Mansion, stick to the side closest to the fireplace to board the ride quicker.

  • A 13 minute wait for Tower of Terror or Haunted Mansion means there is almost no wait to ride.

Tips for Saving Time with the Disney World App

  • Always use Mobile Ordering when available.

  • Click "I'm here" as you're heading to the restaurant. Food will typically come out as you arrive.

  • Use MagicBands and pair your tickets to them.

  • Confirm you party before attempting to enter a virtual queue or buy lightning lanes.

  • Set lightning lane priorities as "Top Picks" in Genie+. This will prevent you from having to scroll to find attractions.

Time Saving Transportation Tips

  • Avoid taking the Minnie Vans and use Lyft or Uber.

  • If staying at a Disney resort, know the bus schedule.

  • Avoid the Skyliner after Epcot Forever. Use a bus or ride share instead.

Money Saving Tips

Disney Gift Card Tips

  • Try to find an exploit to get a discount on Disney Gift Cards, either through credit card or grocery rewards.

  • Link cards to your account on DisneyGiftCard.com.

How to Save on Your Entire Disney World Vacation

  • Look at Costco for Disney World Vacation packages and tickets.

  • Check the special offers at https://disneyworld.disney.go.com/en_CA/special-offers/

How to Save Money on Merch

  • Check out the Disney Character Warehouse Outlet.

How to Save Money on Disney World Dining

  • Get coffee from Joffrey's rather than Starbucks.

  • Split meals between the two of us.

  • Buy a souvenir popcorn bucket and refill it whenever you want for a discounted price.

  • Try free soda samples at Club Cool at Epcot.

  • Try to eat breakfast at the hotel.

  • Bring your own snacks to the park.

  • Ask for a celebration button and wear it around the park.

How to Save on Disney World Resorts

  • Look for a Good Neighbor Hotel.

  • Ask for a better view when checking in.

  • Ask if any upgrades are available.

How to Avoid Overspending at Disney World

  • Bring rain ponchos vs buying them in the park.

  • Counter service restauarants will give you free water.
